When the Only Cure for Motion Sickness is To Keep Moving:

A Travelog of Angels in America

This podcast documents my travel to London, New York, Los Angeles, and San Francisco to identify and index drafts of Tony Kushner's 7-hour-long epic play. My channels house the six episodes of the podcast. Episodes 3, 4, and 5 summarize key changes made to the text throughout its early production history (1987-93). Many of the scenes which are analyzed do not appear in published versions of the play or versions performed now, but they can still dramaturgically inform our readings and productions today. Further acknowledgements can be found at the conclusion of Episode 1.
